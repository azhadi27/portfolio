Clean-Trace UTM Builder

Why this exists

Poorly formatted UTMs lead to fragmented data (e.g., 'Facebook' vs 'facebook'). This tool enforces lowercase consistency and strips special characters to ensure **95% attribution accuracy**[cite: 46, 47].

How to use

  • 1. Enter your landing page URL.
  • 2. Define source, medium, and campaign.
  • 3. The tool automatically sanitizes the text.
  • 4. Use the clean link for your ads/emails.
